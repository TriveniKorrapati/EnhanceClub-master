CIALIS is a prescription medication that is taken orally to help treat erectile dysfunction (ED) and symptoms of benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH).

Cialis

When do you take them?

CIALIS (tadalafil) helps give men the freedom to have sex whenever they want. It lets guys take command of their sexual performance, on-demand. Tadalafil is a prescription pill you swallow at the same time, every day. It helps treat erectile dysfunction (ED) by increasing blood flow to the penis. Together with stimulation, it helps men get an erection and maintain an erection.

Medical Information

Tadalafil is a prescription medication. It’s recommended you follow your doctor’s recommended daily dosage. Some patients may require different doses. Tadalafil does not protect against sexually transmitted diseases, such as HIV. tadalafil helps improve the quality of a stimulated erection. Simply taking tadalafil will not cause an erection.

Tadalafil is also commonly used to help relieve symptoms of benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH). It helps improve a man’s control and frequency of urination.

Recommended dose (per day)

The recommended dose is one tablet (20 mg) before sexual activity, as needed.

Side effects

Some common and uncommon side effects when taking tadalafil include headache, muscle pain and dizziness.  Consult your doctor or pharmacist if you have questions about any side effects. Tadalafil may interfere with other medications or medical conditions, such as heart disease or high blood pressure. Always consult a physician to see if taking CIALIS is right for you.

If you are also taking Finasteride for an enlarged prostate (BPH) consult a physician before taking tadalafil.

What do you feel?

If experiencing an erection lasting 4 hours or more, immediately seek medical attention.  A prolonged erection could cause permanent damage if left untreated. Men should only change their dosage at the direction of a physician. The effects of tadalafil may last up to 36 hours.
