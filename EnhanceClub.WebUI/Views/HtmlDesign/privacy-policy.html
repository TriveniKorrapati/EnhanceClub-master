Summary of Obligations

Pivotal Ventures ("staging.enhanceclub.com") make your wellness our top priority. This Privacy Policy has been established in order to outline our privacy practices as they relate to the provision of health services and practices. You can trust that we will always take reasonable steps to safeguard your personal information.

staging.enhanceclub.com handles all personal information adhering to the terms established in the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act of Canada and the Personal Health Information Act (PHIA) of Manitoba.

  1. Accountability

    staging.enhanceclub.com is responsible for all personal information under its control. staging.enhanceclub.com has an appointed Privacy Officer who oversees its compliance with privacy obligations as well as answering questions or concerns related to our online pharmacy's privacy practices.

    All staging.enhanceclub.com staff have been trained with respect to our privacy obligations.

  2. Identifying Purposes

    The following personal information will be collected from you:

    • Contact information like names, email addresses, mailing addresses, and phone numbers

    • Unique identifiers like user names and passwords

    staging.enhanceclub.com collects, uses, and may disclose your personal information solely for the following purposes, and these are:

    • Administering to your request for health care products or services, and facilitating the provision of them

    • Communication with you regarding the health care products or services you've requested

    • Communicating your personal information to health care professional related to goods or services received through staging.enhanceclub.com. This may includes your Canadian physician, other pharmacists, and other health care professionals. All of this is related to adhering to professional guidelines related to your health care services and products provided online in Canada

    • Following up and providing you with information on staging.enhanceclub.com and our products and services from time to time

    Please note as well that staging.enhanceclub.com will sometimes enter into contract with certain 3rd party suppliers or service providers, including licensed pharmacies in other countries, in the interest of prescription order fulfillment as well as for other specialized services staging.enhanceclub.com may provide your personal information to such 3rd party contractors in order to provide products and or services that cannot be fulfilled here. However, do understand that staging.enhanceclub.com will use contractual means to bind these 3rd party contractors to adhere to our privacy policy obligations detailed above.

    If, for some reason, your personal information is required for a purpose not listed above, we will then contact you to obtain your consent before any such information is disclosed to 3rd party interests.

    Choice / Opt Out

    You are free to choose to stop receiving our newsletter or marketing emails, and can do so by following the unsubscribe instruction included in the email communications or by contacting us at info@enhanceclub.com.

  3. Consent

    staging.enhanceclub.com requires your consent for the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information. The exception to this will by when it is permitted or required by law.

  4. Limiting Collection

    staging.enhanceclub.com will only collect specific information that is required in order to provide services or products being requested, and all info will be collected only fair and entirely lawful means.

    Customers do have the options of choosing for themselves whether they will provide their personal information. However, do be aware that choosing to withhold some types of personal information may limit the nature of the products or services that we are able to provide.

  5. Limiting Use, Disclosure, and Retention

    staging.enhanceclub.com will never use or disclose your personal information for purposes other than for which it was collected. The exception to this will when you have given consent for disclosure of any info or in instanced where the company is required by law to provide it. Also, if staging.enhanceclub.com is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of a all or a portion of our assets then you will be notified via email of this development and any changes that may occur to our privacy policy guidelines should they be changing as a result of the development.

    Please also know that we do no sell personal information to 3rd parties, under any circumstances. If you provide us with personal information about others or others provide information about you then we will only use that information for the specific reason it was provided to us.

  6. Accuracy

    staging.enhanceclub.com will seek confirmation from you that your personal information is accurate and up-to-date for the purposes it will be used for. The pharmacy encourages you to keep your information as updated as possible to ensure our accuracy. Where it is appropriate, will provide notice to 3rd parties who have receive your information to ensure its accuracy when it is essential for the best in customer service.

    Updating / Accessing / Amending / Correcting Personal Information

    Should your personal information change or you no longer with to receive our services then you may correct, update, amend, delete or remove, or ask to have a testimonial taken off our site, or have an account deactivated then you should please email our customer support at info@enhanceclub.com or contact us by telephone or postal mail at the numbers or addresses below:

    Phone: 1-844-836-2582

    Mailing Address:

    Enhance Club, P.O. Box 520, 800 – 15355 24th Avenue, Surrey BC, Canada, V4A 2H9

    We will respond to your requests as soon as we are able to do so.

  7. Safeguards

    staging.enhanceclub.com shall use all appropriate safeguards to protect your personal information. These may include physical measures like locked file cabinets, organizational safeguards where only certain employees may access your personal information, and then technological safeguards like firewalls and passwords.

    Security

    The security of customer's personal information is of the highest importance to us. We adhere to accepted industry standards to protect all personal information submitted to us. This includes during the transmission of it and once we receive it. Do keep in mind, however, that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage method is ever fully 100% secure. As such we cannot completed guarantee the security of information. If you have questions about the security of our online pharmacy website, then please contact us at info@enhanceclub.com

    We offer two means by which you can purchase from our site. You have the option of using our order form, or you can choose a 3rd party payment option. If you are using our shopping cart, the transmission of sensitive credit card payment data will be encrypted using our secure socket layer (SSL) technology. If you choose to use a 3rd party payment option to pay for your order then you should understand that you will be exposed to their privacy policies and security practices as they apply to your information.

  8. Openness

    staging.enhanceclub.com shall make its privacy practices and policies available to individuals when they are requested.

  9. Individual Access

    staging.enhanceclub.com shall inform individuals of the details concerning the existence, use, and disclosure of personal information when it is requested and is require or permitted by law. The individual will receive all access to his or her personal information. All accompanying requests for access to information will be required in writing and must have sufficient detail to allow the company's Privacy Officer to determine the legitimacy of the individual requesting the information. This officer will review all requests and then respond within a period of 30 days, unless the law dictates a different process.

  10. Challenging Compliance

    Individuals can choose to challenge staging.enhanceclub.com's compliance with these privacy principles if they wish. Our Privacy Officer will discuss any questions or concerns you may have. All complaints will be investigated and the pharmacy will also respond to them within a reasonable period of time.

    Changes to this Privacy Policy

    Should we decide to change this privacy policy, we will then post changes to this privacy statement immediately following the changes. This will also be shared on the home page of the pharmacy website, and in other places we think it will be appropriate so that the changes are optimally visible to all customers.

    We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time, so we ask that you review it frequently. If material changes are made to this policy, we will notify you buy email or another means as well as on the home page at staging.enhanceclub.com

  11. Third-Party Advertising

    We use third-party advertising companies to sere ads when you are visiting our website. These companies may use information differently than we do. This information (not including names, email addresses, or telephone numbers – this info will not be available to them) about your visit to site may be used to provide advertisements about good and services of interest to you. Should you wish to have more information about this information and how it is used by these companies, you can contact our customer service team.

    Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies

    staging.enhanceclub.com and our marketing partners, affiliates, analytics or online customer service support will use technologies like cookies, beacons, tags, and scripts, to analyze trends, conduct website administration, track users' movements around the website, as well as gather demographic information about the collective user base. We may receive reports based on the use of these technologies by these companies, and this can be on an individual or aggregated basis.

    Cookies are used to collect non personal information that is then complied for use as aggregated statistic for our shopping cart, to remember users' settings, and for authentication as part of web security. Users can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level. You can reject cookies and may still use our website, but be aware that some features or areas of our website may be limited.

    We use Local Shared Objects, such as Local Storage, like HTML5, to store content information and preferences. Various browsers may offer their own management tools for removing HTML5.

    Analytics / Log Files

    Like most eCommerce websites, we gather certain information automatically and store it in log files. This information includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamp, and clickstream data.

    This information does not identify individual users, and we use it to analyze trends, administer the site, and for helpful tracking of users' movements around the site and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole.

    This automatically-collected data is then linked to personal information. By tying IP addresses to personal information we are able to increase security for the customer, and it makes it even more possible for us to ensure there is no unauthorized access to each customer's account.

    Data Retention: As long as your account remains active, your information will be retained. You do have the option of cancelling your account at any time, or requesting that we no longer use your information Please forward either request to info@enhanceclub.com. We will retain and use your information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

    Links to Other Web Sites

    Included with our website are links to other Web sites whose privacy practices may differ from those of ours at staging.enhanceclub.com. When submitting personal information to any of those sites, please be aware that your information is governed by their privacy policies, and not ours. We encourage you to carefully read the privacy policy of all websites you visit, and whether they are related to personal healthcare or not.

    Social Media Features and Widgets

    Social media features will be found on our website, and one example is the Facebook Like button. These features may collect your IP address, the pages you visit on our site, and setting a Cookie may be required to enable the Feature to function properly. Social Media Features and Widgets are either hosted by a 3rd party or hosted directly on our Site. Be aware that your interactions with these features are governed according to the privacy policy of the company providing it, and not those of Enhance Club.

    Testimonials

    Personal testimonials of satisfied customers will be displayed on our site, in addition to other 1st-person external endorsements. With your consent we may post your testimonial, and your name will be posted along with it. As mentioned above, you can request to have a submitted testimonial removed.

    Referrals

    If you choose to use our referral service to tell a friend about our site, you will be asked for that person's name and email address. We will automatically send your friend a one-time email inviting him or her to visit the site. staging.enhanceclub.com stores this information, but only for the purpose of sending this one-time email [and tracking the success of our referral program]. Your referred persons may contact us at info@enhanceclub.com and ask that we remove this information from our database. All such requests will be accommodated and the information will be deleted.

  12. Collecting Data

    Do note as well that we have the ability to retain any data that is provided to us by you, and this can include that which is submitted even if you did not complete registration or placed your order officially by submitting in the online checkout. These contact details and data may be used to contact you and inquire why you chose not to complete the registration process OR finalize your purchases from staging.enhanceclub.com
